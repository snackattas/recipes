Ingredients:
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt
 - 3 tablespoons cornstarch
 - 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
 - sesame oil
 - vegetable oil
 - Extra firm tofu

Directions:
  1. Press the tofu for 30 minutes. Then let it rest
  2. Cut the tofu into bite size chunks  
  3. Toss it in all those ingredients and roast @ 400 degrees for 40 minutes
